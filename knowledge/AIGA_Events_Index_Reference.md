## Event Categories

AoEM events fall into five categories:
1. **Individual Events** — scored per player (MGE, MEE)
2. **Alliance Events** — alliance-wide participation
3. **Routine Events** — recurring scheduled events
4. **Bonus Events** — limited-time special events
5. **Store Events** — purchase-based progression systems

---

## Individual Events

### Mightiest Governor (MGE)
**Type:** Individual competitive event | **Duration:** 7 days (1 day per stage)
**Full documentation:** See AIGA_System_Prompt and AIGA_MGE_Reference

**Stage summary:**

| Day | Primary activity | Key scoring actions |
|---|---|---|
| I | Tribal Raid | Tribe lv29-30 = 300 pts, lv25-28 = 260 pts |
| II | Hero Growth | Legendary gear craft = 15,000 pts, Legendary medal = 2,500 pts |
| III | Gather Resources + Wheel | Wheel spin = 1,000 pts, 100 resources = 1 pt |
| IV | Race Against Time | Meteor Steel = 20,000 pts, Fine Gold = 3,000 pts, speedup = 30 pts/min |
| V | Unit Training | T7 = 100 pts, T6 = 50 pts, T4 = 10 pts per troop (fresh only) |
| VI | Power Boost | 2 pts per 1 power gained |
| VII | Go All Out | All activities combined |

**AIGA save rules:** Legendary medals → Day II | Wheel spins → Day III | Training speedups → Day V or MEE | Building/research speedups → Day IV+VI or MEE | Never promote during MGE

---

### Mightiest Empire (MEE)
**Type:** Alliance competitive event | **Duration:** Multi-day
**Full documentation:** See AIGA_System_Prompt and AIGA_MEE_Reference

**Key multipliers vs MGE:**
- Tribe defeat: 80x higher scoring than MGE
- Speedups: 600x higher than MGE — highest leverage activity
- Training: 10x higher than MGE
- 1 day of speedups = approximately 25.9M MEE points

**AIGA priority:** MEE is the highest-stakes event in the game. Save speedup pools specifically for MEE timing. Coordinate with alliance on speedup burn timing.

## Alliance Events

### Supplies Bounty
**Type:** Alliance | **Trigger:** Supply Depot required
**Mechanic:** Challenge increasingly difficult boss tiers on the world map. Each completed level generates passive storage of speedups and resources at the Supply Depot, up to 12 hours of passive storage. Claim at the depot.
**AIGA note:** Consistent daily passive income source. Always progress to the highest available level.

---

### Trojan Turmoil
**Type:** Alliance | **Frequency:** Every 2 days | **Duration:** 30 minutes | **Started by:** Alliance leader
**Mechanic:** Three waves of Trojan Horse enemies appear near the Supply Depot. Each wave has a different unit type weakness. Rally against each — 7min 30s per wave before auto-advancing.
**Rewards:** Up to 2,000 Empire Coins every 2 days + resources + War Hall blueprints
**AIGA note:** Empire Coins are scarce and valuable. This is one of the best free sources. Never miss it. Use unit counter wheel to maximise damage on each wave.

### Rally Against Tribes
**Type:** Alliance competitive | **Scope:** Server-wide
**Mechanic:** Alliance competes for server rankings by accumulating damage against tribes. Prizes awarded for alliances that reach certain damage thresholds.
**AIGA note:** Alliance-level stamina coordination maximises ranking. High-stamina players should focus tribe attacks during this event. Relevant for alliance leaders managing attack scheduling.

---

### Giants Roar
**Type:** Alliance | **Duration:** Early game only — limited window
**Mechanic:** Defeat common tribes to collect Giant's Treasure banners. Use banners to summon Giant Legions. Rally alliance against them.
**King Derrick medal limits:** No daily cap on your own banners. 30 medals/day cap while assisting other players.
**AIGA note:** Primary early-game source of King Derrick medals. Do not miss — event does not return after the early game window closes.

## Bonus Events

### Legendary Advent (Wheel)
**Type:** Bonus | **Frequency:** Approximately every 1-2 weeks | **Duration:** 3 days
**Full documentation:** See AIGA_System_Prompt

**Key facts:**
- 8 free spins per event — use every day without fail
- Single spin: 900 Empire Coins | Pack: check current pricing in-game
- Each event features a specific hero rotation
- 20 spins guarantees a chest with 10 hero medals — minimum to summon a new hero
- Save paid spins for MGE Day III (1,000 pts per spin)
- New hero launches always trigger a Legendary Advent
- Max 2,000 spins per event session

**Milestone bonuses at:** 20 / 60 / 120 / 200 / 300 / 400 / 500 / 600 / 800 / 1,000 spins

## Store Events

These are purchase-based systems, not competitive events. AIGA does not advise on real-money spending. Listed for completeness and to help players understand what these menus contain.

| Store Event | Description |
|---|---|
| Hero Growth Speed Up | Limited-time randomly refreshed hero development packs |
| Value Packs | Gift packs containing resources, hero development items, speedups |
| Prize Frenzy | Chain packs — free pack unlocked between purchases |
| Hero Rally | Purchase hero fragments using Hero Coins |
| Daily Special | Daily refresh gift packs |
| Limited-Time Bounty | Short-term battle pass (free tier + paid deluxe) |
| Kingdom's Hoard | Long-term battle pass (free tier + paid deluxe) |
| Top-Up Bonus | Rewards for accumulating Empire Coin purchases (cumulative thresholds up to 5,000 coins) |
| Regular Supplies | Weekly and monthly login reward cards |
| Growth Fund | TC upgrade gift pack |
| VIP Store | Unlocks progressively with VIP level — see AIGA_VIP_Reference |

**AIGA F2P note on Kingdom's Hoard:** The free tier of the Kingdom's Hoard is valuable and should be completed daily. The paid tier is outside AIGA's advisory scope.
