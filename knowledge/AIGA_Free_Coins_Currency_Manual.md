# AIGA — Free Coins & Currency Manual
## Age of Empires Mobile
### Network Grey | Version 1.0 | April 2026
### Scope: Free-to-play coin sources, spend priorities, and cadence only

---

## Overview

AoEM has **8 distinct currencies**. Each has its own earn mechanic, store, and reset cadence. Empire Coins are the master benchmark — everything else can be evaluated against them.

**The golden rule for all secondary currencies:** They build up whether you spend them or not. Every store resets weekly. Unspent coins before reset are value lost. Always spend before reset.

**The master currency.** Everything in the game can be benchmarked against EC.

### Value benchmark

### Free sources

| Source | EC earned | Cadence | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Daily login rewards | *[verify in-game]* | Daily | Small but compounds over time |
| Achievements | *[verify in-game]* | One-time | Check achievement list regularly |

**AIGA priority note:** Trojan Turmoil (up to 2,000 EC every 2 days) is the most impactful repeatable free source. Never miss it. Requires alliance leader to start — coordinate with SirCarmo.

### Where to spend EC (free EC only — priority order)

| Priority | Spend | Cost | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Legendary Advent wheel (MGE Day III only) | 900/spin or 4,200/10-pack | 1,000 MGE pts per spin + hero medals. 10-pack saves 4,800 EC vs singles |
| 2 | Legendary Skill Scrolls | 1,000 each | Only if critically needed before a major event. Prefer earning via VIP |

**Never spend EC on:** Resources (5 EC/1,000 — gathering is always better), Guard Tower blueprints, or anything available cheaper in secondary currency stores.

**Free EC ceiling:** As a free player, EC is strictly limited. Once free sources are exhausted, further EC requires purchase. Treat every EC as scarce.

## 2. Alliance Coins

**Earned by:** Donating to alliance (up to 20 donations/day), assisting alliance members (up to 20 assists/day), participating in alliance events.

**Important distinction:** Alliance Coins in the Treasury are *your personal coins* — not the alliance's shared funds. The alliance's shared resources are in the Warehouse, not the Treasury. These are yours to spend.

### Free earn sources

| Source | Cadence | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Alliance donations | Daily (20 max) | Do all 20 every day |
| Alliance assists | Daily (20 max) | Do all 20 every day — also reduces allies' queue times |
| Alliance events (Trojan Turmoil, etc.) | Event cadence | Additional coin rewards on top of EC |
| Tribal Vanguard rallies | Daily/weekly | No stamina cost — always participate |

## 3. Tactic Coins (Island Tactics)

**Earned by:** Passive production based on your Island Tactics progression level. The further you progress through the 900 stages, the more coins you earn per hour.

**Collection cap: 12 hours.** Production stops accumulating after 12 hours. Miss a collection and you lose coins permanently.

### Free earn mechanic
- Progress Island Tactics stages to increase your passive earn rate
- Coins accumulate in the background 24/7 up to the 12-hour cap
- Collect twice daily minimum (every ~10-12 hours) — set a reminder

### Where to spend (priority order)

| Priority | Item | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Iron Meteorite | Required for ring Tier 1 and 2 upgrades. Buy every reset |
| 2 | Forging Tools | Required for gear levelling. You currently have 0 — critical gap |
| 3 | Magma Crystals | Required for gear star upgrades |
| 4 | Gear blueprints | Progress through rarity tiers as store unlocks improve |
| 5 | Skill Points | Secondary source after Alliance Treasury |
| 6 | Forging speedups | Speed up gear levelling queues |

**Non-negotiable:** Buy every gear material item in the Island Tactics store every weekly reset. This is the best and most consistent free source of gear crafting materials in the game.

**Reset:** Weekly. Collect coins daily, spend store weekly.

## 5. Merits

**Earned by:** Combat — fighting tribes and PvP activity. Merits accumulate permanently and never reset. They represent your lifetime combat record.

**AIGA note:** Merits also serve as a proxy indicator of combat experience. High merits relative to power suggests an active fighter.

|---|---|---|
| Tribe defeats | Every stamina spend | Primary source |
| PvP combat | Open field/rally | Earns merits alongside other rewards |

## 7. Gleaming Coins

**Earned by:** Giants Roar event (early game only, limited window). Defeat common tribes to collect Giant's Treasure banners, summon Giant Legions, rally alliance against them.

**Critical warning:** Giants Roar is an early-game event with a limited window. It does not return after the early game period closes. This is the primary free source of King Derrick medals.

### Where to spend

**AIGA note:** If you are still in the early game window, prioritise this event above most others. King Derrick is a strong hero and these medals will not be available again through this channel.

## Daily & Weekly Coin Routine

| Task | Currency impact |
|---|---|
| Collect Island Tactics coins (x2, ~10-12h apart) | Prevents production cap loss |
