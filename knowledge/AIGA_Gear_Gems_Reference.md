| Rarity | Iron Meteorite cost | Craft time | MGE Day II score |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rare | 150 | 2h | 1,000 pts |
| Epic | 400 | 6h | 5,000 pts |
| Legendary | 3,000 | 39h 59m (~40h) | 30,000 pts |

**Key rule:** Do not use crafting speedups on Legendary gear until Smithy reaches level 25 (36% speed reduction). At Smithy lv25 Legendary craft time reduces to approximately 25-26 hours.

## Gear Dismantling — Kits Returned

Dismantling gear returns Targeted Forge kits based on rarity:

| Rarity | Kits returned |
|---|---|
| Rare | 50 |
| Epic | 250 |
| Legendary | 600 |

**Rule:** Always dismantle Rare gear immediately — 50 kits returned is more valuable than an equipped Rare piece. Never dismantle Epic or Legendary without resetting first.

## Gear Milestone Levels — Strategic Importance

| Level | What unlocks |
|---|---|
| 10 | First gem slot (all slots) |
| 20 | Star upgrade available (requires duplicate piece) |
| 30 | Second gem slot (Head/Hands/Body), requires Legendary for most pieces |
| 40 | Third gem slot (Head/Hands/Body), Rare gear max level |
| 60 | Third gem slot (Legs only), Epic gear max level |
| 80 | Legendary gear max level |

**Priority rule:** Push all four pieces on a hero to lv10 before pushing any piece to lv20. Gem slots at lv10 give disproportionate value across all four slots simultaneously.

## Smithy Speed Bonus by Level

Higher Smithy level reduces all gear crafting time by this percentage.

| Smithy level | Speed bonus |
|---|---|
| 1 | 2% |
| 5 | 10% |
| 10 | 21% |
| 15 | 36% |
| 20 | 56% |
| 25 | 78% |
| 30 | 101% (effectively ~50% of base time) |

**Key milestone:** Smithy lv15 gives 36% speed reduction. This is the threshold where Legendary gear crafting becomes practical for active players. Do not use speedups on Legendary gear before reaching Smithy lv15 minimum.

## Gem System

Gems are inserted into gear slots once unlocked. Three gem categories exist.

### Gem categories

**Strategy gems** — boost troop stats:
- Unit health
- Unit defense
- Unit attack
- Unit capacity
- Gathering speed

**Hero gems** — boost hero stats:
- Might
- Strategy
- Armor
- Siege
- All attributes

**Tactic gems** — boost skill damage:
- Passive skill damage
- Secondary strike skill damage
- Active skill damage
- Turn-based skill damage
- Healing effects
- Normal attack damage

## Gem Recommendations by Hero Role

| Hero role | Head (Strategy) | Hands (Hero) | Body (Tactic) | Legs (Mixed) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sword attack lead (Musashi) | Unit attack | Might | Active skill damage | Unit attack / Might / Active |
| Support/buff (Hammurabi) | Unit health | All attributes | Healing | Unit defense / Armor / Healing |
| Recovery support | Unit health | Armor | Healing | Unit health / Armor / Healing |
| Pike lead (Joan) | Unit attack | Might or Strategy | Active or Turn-based | Unit attack / Might / Active |
| Cavalry lead | Unit attack | Might | Active or Secondary | Unit attack / Might / Active |
| Archer lead | Unit attack | Strategy | Active or Passive | Unit attack / Strategy / Active |
| Gathering hero | Gathering speed | Any | Any | Gathering / Any / Any |

**General priority:** Avoid Siege gems on any combat hero. Unit capacity gems (Strategy category) are excellent for march leads as they directly increase troop count per march.

## Gear Investment Priority Summary

1. Get all 4 pieces to lv10 on M1 heroes first — unlocks first gem slot on all pieces
2. Then push all 4 pieces to lv20 on M1 — unlocks star upgrades
3. Repeat for M2 heroes before touching M3/M4/M5
4. Insert gems as soon as slots unlock — even tier 1 gems provide immediate benefit
5. Never dismantle Epic or Legendary — reset to recover materials instead
6. Rare gear: dismantle immediately for 50 kits each
