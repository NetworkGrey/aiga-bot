## Gear Stat Categories by Slot

Each gear slot provides a consistent set of stat categories across all troop types. No exact values are provided on the wiki — for max-level stat values use individual gear pages when available.

### Head Slot (all troop types)
- Troop-specific defence bonus up
- Troop-specific health bonus up
- Unit capacity up
- Damage taken from counter unit type down
- *(Legendary tier only)* Troop-specific damage taken down

### Hands Slot (all troop types)
- Troop-specific attack bonus up
- Troop-specific damage up
- Base elimination rate during siege up
- Damage dealt to counter unit type up
- *(Selected pieces only)* Skill damage up — see table below

### Body Slot (all troop types)
- Troop-specific defence bonus up
- Troop-specific health bonus up
- Unit capacity up
- Damage taken from counter unit type down
- *(Legendary tier only)* Troop-specific damage taken down

### Legs Slot (all troop types)
- Troop-specific attack bonus up
- Troop-specific damage up
- Elimination rate up
- Damage dealt to counter unit type up
- *(Selected pieces only)* Skill damage up — see table below

## Advisory Notes for AIGA

**Skill damage gear matters for skill-heavy heroes:**
- Miyamoto Musashi (Sword) — Flowing Force (Legendary hands) + Shadowless Wind (Legendary legs) provide skill damage. Both are confirmed on his current gear. Correct.
- Joan of Arc (Pike) — Ring of Chaos (Legendary hands) is the skill damage hands piece for pike. Her current Legacy Arm Armor does not have skill damage — upgrade path to Ring of Chaos is a priority for skill-damage builds.
- Guan Yu (Cavalry) — Dust-Free Boots (Legendary legs) confirmed in his current gear. Contract of Light hands — skill damage status unconfirmed *[verify in-game]*.
- Yi Sun-Shin (Archer) — Embrace of Effulgence is the skill damage hands for archers. Currently on Skyshatter Gauntlets (no skill damage). Upgrade priority.

**Counter damage stats are more valuable than they appear:**
- Head and Body gear reduce damage from the unit type that counters you
- Hands and Legs gear boost damage against the unit type you counter
- These stats are baked into all gear at every tier — not optional additions. They passively amplify the counter system's 30% damage differential.

**Elimination rate in siege:**
- Hands pieces note "base elimination rate during siege" — relevant for city attack players
- Legs pieces note "elimination rate up" (general)
- Low strategic value for field combat — siege stat category
