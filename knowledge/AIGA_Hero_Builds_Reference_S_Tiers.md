### Lu Bu
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Cavalry | **Specialties:** Warrior, Attack, Critical Strike

**Skill Build (Cavalry):**
- Skill 1: Wrath Forged Blade (commander)
- Skill 2: Shadow of Terror (signature)
- Skill 3: Ripper Tiger
- Skill 4: Critical Insight

**Pairings:** Guan Yu, Attila the Hun, Harald

**Gear:** Divine Crown, Omniscience, Contract of Light, Dust-Free Boots

**Rings:** Radiant Guardian, Ring of Deer, Ring of Clover

**Gems:** Hero's secondary strike skill damage up, Hero's might up, All unit types' attack up

**Notes:** Signature skill adds Deterrence stacks that increase might. Critical synergy hero — Critical Insight pairs with Ripper Tiger to keep building crit rate. High single-target damage output.

- Skill 2: Light of Mithra (signature)

## S Tier Heroes

---

### Sun Tzu
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Swordsmen | **Specialties:** Tactician, Attack, Charging

**Skill Build (Swordsmen):**
- Skill 1: Strategy Cuts Deeper (commander)
- Skill 2: The Way of War (signature)
- Skill 3: Flash of Inspiration
- Skill 4: Strategy Master's Gift

**Pairings:** Philip IV, Theodora, Cleopatra, Baldassi, Nino

**Notes:** Ultimate Strategist state buffs next strategy skills. Flash of Inspiration gives 17.17% chance to skip charge — pairs well with charging signature. Strategy Master's Gift boosts both strategy damage and activation chance. Strong strategy-damage swordsmen lead, especially paired with Theodora or Philip for synergy.

---

### Ramesses II
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Swordsmen, Archer | **Specialties:** Tactician, Attack, Active

**Skill Build (Swordsmen):**
- Skill 1: Son of Ra (commander)
- Skill 2: Storms of Set (signature)
- Skill 3: Flame of Genesis
- Skill 4: High Spirit

**Pairings:** Charlemagne, Theodora, Sun Tzu, Philip IV

**Notes:** Divine Protection state activates signature once per extra second — extremely high activation rate. Storms of Set recovers extra rage per activation for the first 30s. Flame of Genesis stacks strategy up to 6 times per battle. High Spirit and Flame of Genesis compound the activation advantage. Rage-generation specialist.

---

### Octavian
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Pikemen | **Specialties:** Marshal, Attack, Turn Base

**Skill Build (Pikemen):**
- Skill 1: Accumulated Power (commander)
- Skill 2: Might of Ruler (signature)
- Skill 3: King's Blade
- Skill 4: Whirlwind Sweep

**Pairings:** Leonidas, Julius Caesar, Bushra

**Notes:** Supreme Mark mechanic — 8 secondary strike/turn-based activations consume stacks for +8.94% damage for 9s. Commander skill adds 2 extra stacks per trigger. Coordination skill reduces normal attack damage but boosts turn-based — only use if running a turn-based focused build. Whirlwind Sweep stacks to 5x over time. Strong sustained pikemen lead with turn-based synergy.

---

### Mehmed II
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Archer, Swordsmen | **Specialties:** Warrior, Support, Buff

**Skill Build (Archer/Swordsmen):**
- Skill 1: Sultan Caesar (commander)
- Skill 2: Fatih (signature)
- Skill 3: Owl's Lament
- Skill 4: Peaceful Haven

**Pairings:** Attila, Bellevue, Yodit, Tribhuwana

**Notes:** Commander boosts nearby allied troop damage and converts incoming skill damage to unit recovery for 9s. Fatih gives 60% chance every 6s to reduce damage taken AND boost active/secondary strike skills simultaneously. Owl's Lament provides persistent recovery. Peaceful Haven reduces damage taken after each recovery trigger. Strong defensive support hero for mixed archer/swordsmen marches.

### Suleiman
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Archer | **Specialties:** Tactician, Attack, Charging

**Skill Build (Archer):**
- Skill 1: Law of Kanus (commander)
- Skill 2: Magnificent Conquest (signature)
- Skill 3: Conflagration
- Skill 4: Strategy Master's Gift

**Pairings:** Queen Seondeok, Theodora, Yi Sun-Shin, Yuan Xia

**Notes:** Magnificent Conquest deals extra effects based on enemy debuff state — Burn triggers extra damage, Silence boosts next skill, Incapacitation recovers units. Pairs with Conflagration (burn) and Blaze Impact to pre-apply burn before signature fires. Law of Kanus applies incapacitation directly. Strategy Master's Gift boosts both strategy damage and signature activation. Top-tier archer strategy damage lead.

### Queen Seondeok
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Archer | **Specialties:** Tactician, Support, Active

**Skill Build (Archer):**
- Skill 1: Fight Fire with Fire (commander)
- Skill 2: Halo of Hwangnyong (signature)
- Skill 3: Conflagration
- Skill 4: War Tactic

**Pairings:** Suleiman, Theodora, Yi Sun-Shin, Yuan Xia

**Notes:** Commander increases enemy strategy damage taken by 5.96% for 6s — a debuff that amplifies the entire march. Signature has 23.84% chance to silence enemy (blocks commander/active skills). Conflagration applies burn DoT. War Tactic deals damage AND recovers units simultaneously. Ideal second-in-command alongside Suleiman — the silence + burn + damage taken debuff combo is devastating.

### Saladin
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Cavalry, Archer | **Specialties:** Marshal, Support, Rage Recovery

**Skill Build (Cavalry):**
- Skill 1: Triple Clash (commander)
- Skill 2: Fury of the Sands (signature)
- Skill 3: Roar of Victory
- Skill 4: Weak Spot Attack

**Pairings:** El Cid, Bushra, Attila, Axel

**Notes:** Unique rage recovery speciality — signature generates 0.45 rage per second passively, plus extra rage on each skill activation (triggers every 3s max). Triple Clash hits 3 enemy troops. Particularly strong for keeping rage at maximum for commander skill spam. Works well with Tomyris (who also benefits from high-frequency skill activation).

- Skill 2: Valkyrie's Edge (signature)
- Skill 3: Raging Bloodline
- Skill 4: High Spirit

Berserker's Shield Dance boosts signature activation chance for 6s. Raging Bloodline after 3 signature actives boosts highest-might hero and reduces crit damage taken. High Spirit increases activation chance further. Defense-penetration focused swordsmen lead.

- Skill 1: Regal Radiance (commander)
- Skill 2: Ode to Java (signature)
- Skill 3: Righteous Judgement

### Mansa Musa
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Pikemen | **Specialties:** Warrior, Support, Buff

**Skill Build (Pikemen):**
- Skill 1: Spend a Fortune (commander)
- Skill 2: Devout Radiance (signature)
- Skill 3: Golden Odyssey
- Skill 4: Fearless Retribution

**Pairings:** Richard, Boudica, Leonidas

**Notes:** Spend a Fortune reduces enemy commander armor by 20.86 for 6s — high debuff value. Devout Radiance triggers on 12 incoming normal attacks (up to 2/s) — deals damage and reduces damage taken. Golden Odyssey similarly triggers on 12 incoming attacks but recovers units and boosts commander damage. Fearless Retribution adds counter + recovery. Strong defensive pikemen support, best used alongside other counterattack heroes (Richard, Boudica).

### Boudica
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Pikemen | **Specialties:** Warrior, Attack, Counterattack

**Skill Build (Pikemen):**
- Skill 1: Sawtooth Pike (commander)
- Skill 2: Swift Reprisal (signature)
- Skill 3: King's Blade
- Skill 4: Forceful Retaliation

**Pairings:** Richard, Frederick, Joan of Arc, Darius

**Notes:** Commander deals AoE to 3 troops AND recovers units simultaneously. Swift Reprisal counters at 20% chance on hit and passively boosts highest-might hero's might in battle. King's Blade adds a third counterattack layer. Forceful Retaliation adds might buff on counter trigger. Three counterattack mechanics running simultaneously — one of the strongest counter-focused builds in the game.

---

### Ram Khamhaeng
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Pikemen | **Specialties:** Marshal, Buff, Turn Base

**Skill Build (Pikemen):**
- Skill 1: Valor of Rama (commander)
- Skill 2: Splendor of Sukhothai (signature)
- Skill 3: Savage
- Skill 4: War Elephant

**Pairings:** Octavian, Bushra, Julius Caesar

**Notes:** Unique rally-support mechanic — Splendor of Sukhothai boosts all nearby allied commanders' highest attribute by 4 per 6s up to 6 stacks for 3 minutes. This is an alliance-wide buff in rallies (within 1,500 range, requires 80%+ unit capacity). Valor of Rama puts up to 3 allied troops into relaxation state for a damage boost that converts to recovery when the state ends. Savage reduces enemy armor. War Elephant reduces incoming skill damage. Best value in alliance rally contexts.

---

### Yodit
**Rarity:** Legendary | **Unit:** Swordsmen | **Specialties:** Warrior, Attack, Active

**Skill Build (Swordsmen):**
- Skill 1: Wrath Forged Blade (commander)
- Skill 2: Shadow of Terror (signature)
- Skill 3: Executor
- Skill 4: Battle Roar

**Pairings:** Miyamoto Musashi, Tribhuwana, Constantine, Hammurabi

**Notes:** Deterrence stack mechanic — each signature activation adds 1 stack (+8.94 might per stack, up to 4). At 4 stacks, commander skill changes from 3 hits to 5 hits. Shadow of Terror has small (2.98%) chance to enter damage immunity for 1s. Executor boosts activation chance and adds 10.10% chance per activation for +16.16% skill damage. Battle Roar gives sustained damage buff for 6s. Strong snowball hero — gets better as fight continues.
