## S2→S3 BLENDING RULES

1. Attila stays with Lu Bu. Mehmed II goes to Mulan march.
2. Mansa Musa replaces Richard in W.PIK. Leonidas stays as lead.
3. Yodit slots into Miyamoto as 2IC, then Lagertha as 3rd when available.
4. Ramesses = open field only, rank5 required, never rally.
5. Mehmed II priority pickup — works W.ARC and W.SW.
6. Charlemagne replaces Philip IV entirely. Philip has no use post-S3.
7. Ring of Daisy confirmed > Radiant Guardian on Lu Bu (battle tested).
8. Radiant Guardian on Mulan = essential for spender archer march.
9. Richard still usable but outclassed — Leonidas preferred lead in S3.
