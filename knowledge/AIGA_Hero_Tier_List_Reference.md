## AIGA Source Document v1.0

## Important Disclaimer

Tier lists reflect general hero potential under optimal conditions with full investment. A lower-tier hero with maximum investment (level 100, maxed skills, developed gear) will outperform a higher-tier hero with minimal investment. Always evaluate heroes in the context of a player's actual account state, not tier alone.

Tier ratings change with game updates and meta shifts. Verify against current community sources when advising on new hero investments.

---

## Tier Definitions

| Tier | Meaning |
|---|---|
| S+ | Very overpowered — strongest heroes in game |
| S | Overpowered — excellent in most situations |
| A | Balanced — strong heroes worth investing in |
| B | Slightly underpowered — usable but better options exist |
| C | Underpowered — invest only if no better option available |
| D | Worst — avoid investment |

## Key Investment Notes by Hero

### Heroes that outperform their tier with investment
- **Joan of Arc (C-tier):** With lv95+, maxed skills and Rank 3+, performs well as pike rally lead due to counterattack kit. Do not let tier rating discourage investment if she is your best pike lead.
- **Hammurabi (C-tier):** With maxed buff skills performs very well as M1 sword support. Sage King and Authoritative Code are genuinely strong.
- **Wu Wei (C-tier):** With lv95 and Tit for Tat maxed, strong counterattack contribution to pike march.
- **Gatos (C-tier):** With lv95 and secondary strike skills, solid M2 pike support.
- **Darius the Great (C-tier):** Rank 3/4 at low level still adds base stat value. Long-term development asset.
- **King Derick (A-tier):** Rank 5 significantly elevates performance above tier rating — one of the strongest per-rank heroes available.

### Heroes rated higher than they perform for most players
- **Yi Seong-Gye (A-tier):** Only viable as cavalry lead. Early game usable but quickly outclassed.
- **Sejong the Great (A-tier):** Recovery skills are weak compared to S-tier supports. Use as placeholder until better cavalry support available.

### Gathering specialists — do not invest universal resources
- **Diao Chan:** Best gathering lead. Signature skill gives 1.5x daily resources. Never use in combat march. Barbarian grind only for levels.
- **Cleopatra:** Good gathering support. Dual cavalry/sword type gives flexibility. Transitions to combat support when fully developed.
- **Darius the Great:** Efficient Harvest skill makes him a solid gathering support. Dual type gives march flexibility.
- **Bushra, Leon, Narses, Kaso, Nino, Baldassi:** Pure gathering heroes — no combat investment needed.
