## Hero Rank — Medal Requirements

Rank upgrades require medals specific to each hero. Medals are earned from events, Legendary Advent wheel, exploration and alliance activities.

| Rank | Medals required | Cumulative medals |
|---|---|---|
| Rank 1 | 10 | 10 |
| Rank 2 | 20 | 30 |
| Rank 3 | 50 | 80 |
| Rank 4 | 100 | 180 |
| Rank 5 | 150 | 330 |
| Rank 6 | 270 | 600 |

**Maximum rank: 6** (requires 600 total medals for that specific hero)

**Strategic rule:** Rank directly boosts base stats. A Rank 3 hero at lv30 can outperform a Rank 1 hero at lv60 in base stats. Accumulate medals consistently — never discard hero medals.

## Skill Star Upgrades — Scroll Requirements

Skill stars unlock at certain skill levels and require scrolls to activate. Two scroll types: Epic and Legendary.

| Star | Scrolls required |
|---|---|
| Star 1 | 20 |
| Star 2 | 50 |
| Star 3 | 100 |
| Star 4 | 150 |
| Star 5 | 270 |

**Maximum stars: 5** (requires 270 scrolls for star 5)

**Strategic rule:** Skill stars multiply the base effectiveness of skills. Higher star skills on fully maxed heroes are the ultimate long-term power investment. Save scrolls for primary march lead heroes only.

## XP Tome Denominations and Efficiency

| Tome | XP granted | Relative efficiency |
|---|---|---|
| 500 XP | 500 | Baseline |
| 1,000 XP | 1,000 | Same |
| 10,000 XP | 10,000 | Same |
| 50,000 XP | 50,000 | Same |
| 100,000 XP | 100,000 | Best for large pushes |

All tomes grant exactly their stated XP — no efficiency difference between denominations. Use larger tomes for larger pushes to reduce the number of individual actions required.
