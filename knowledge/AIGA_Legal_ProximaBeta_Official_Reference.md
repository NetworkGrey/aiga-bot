## Who We Are Dealing With

Age of Empires Mobile is developed by **TiMi Studio Group** and published by **Level Infinite**, both subsidiaries of **Tencent Games**. The legal entity responsible for the service is **Proxima Beta Pte. Limited**, registered in Singapore.

The game IP itself — Age of Empires name, franchise and related marks — is owned by **Microsoft / Xbox Game Studios / World's Edge**. The game is © / TM / ® 2024 Microsoft.

This means AIGA must respect obligations to two separate IP holders:
- **Proxima Beta / Level Infinite** — game service, content, mechanics, data
- **Microsoft / Xbox** — Age of Empires brand, franchise name, legacy IP

## Privacy Policy — Key Points for AIGA

**Source:** https://aoemobile.com/policy/pr-lang/en.html (last updated 2026-01-29)

### Data controller
Proxima Beta Pte. Limited, 10 Anson Road #21-07, International Plaza, Singapore 079903.

### What data the game collects from players
The game collects extensive player data including device identifiers, gameplay data, transaction history, chat data, VIP status, login patterns, frequently used heroes, purchasing history and analytics. Chat data is stored only on device and not on servers.

### Third-party analytics
The game uses Firebase (Google), AppsFlyer, Facebook Analytics and Adjust for user analytics and ad attribution. Data is stored on servers in the US and Singapore.

### Children
Service is restricted to users 18+ in most markets including South Africa, Australia, Indonesia, Malaysia and most of Asia. South Africa minimum age is explicitly listed as 18.

### South Africa specifically
South African users have the right to lodge complaints with the Information Regulator (South Africa) at inforeg@justice.gov.za.

### Data rights
Users can request access, correction, erasure and portability of data by contacting Proxima Beta through the official DSAR form at aoemobile.com.

## End User License Agreement — Key Points for AIGA

**Source:** Proxima Beta EULA (standard across all Proxima Beta / Level Infinite games)

### Ownership of game content
All game content — heroes, mechanics, game systems, visual design, names and related materials — is owned by Proxima Beta / Microsoft. Players receive a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use the game. Players own nothing in the game, including virtual goods purchased with real money.

### What the EULA restricts
The EULA prohibits:
- Reverse engineering, modifying or decompiling the game
- Using automation, bots or any tool to modify gameplay
- Exploiting bugs or vulnerabilities for advantage
- Buying, selling or transferring accounts
- Any commercial use of game content without authorisation

### Third-party tools and fan content
The EULA does not explicitly prohibit fan-made advisory tools, guides or strategy communities. However, it does prohibit any tool that automates gameplay or interacts with game data or servers in unauthorised ways.

### Feedback clause
If users provide feedback to Proxima Beta, the company is free to use that feedback without compensation. Not directly relevant to AIGA but noted.

### Governing law
For users outside the EU/UK, disputes are resolved through binding arbitration in Singapore. For South African users, this means legal recourse is through Singapore arbitration, not South African courts.

## IP and Copyright — What Applies to AIGA

### Microsoft / Xbox — Age of Empires franchise
- The Age of Empires name and brand are Microsoft trademarks
- Microsoft has a published Fan Content Policy (Xbox Game Studios) that permits fan-made non-commercial content referencing their games
- Fan strategy guides, community tools and advisory services generally fall within the spirit of permitted fan content — provided they are not misleading about official affiliation and do not monetise the IP directly
- AIGA must clearly state it is not affiliated with or endorsed by Microsoft, Xbox, TiMi or Level Infinite

### Proxima Beta / Level Infinite — game service and content
- Game mechanics, hero names, and game systems are proprietary
- No explicit fan content policy has been published by Proxima Beta for AoEM specifically
- Advisory tools that help players understand official game mechanics are standard in the gaming industry and widely tolerated — AIGA falls in this category
- The critical distinction: AIGA advises players on how to play; it does not reproduce game assets, automate gameplay, or circumvent monetisation

### No-go areas for AIGA
- Never claim affiliation with TiMi, Level Infinite, Proxima Beta, Microsoft or Xbox
- Never reproduce game artwork, character art or UI screenshots in any AIGA product
- Never reproduce in-game text verbatim at length (hero descriptions, item descriptions etc)
- Never advise players on how to exploit bugs, use unauthorised modifications, or circumvent the game's payment systems
- Never build tools that interact with game servers or automate any in-game action

## AIGA Compliance Assessment

### What AIGA can do
- Provide strategic advice on official game mechanics
- Reference hero names, troop types, building names and game systems as game facts
- Operate as a fan advisory service — this is standard and widely practised across all major mobile strategy games
- Charge players for access to advice (this is AIGA's own service, not selling game content)
- Use hero names and troop types in reference documents and responses

### What AIGA cannot do
- Claim any affiliation with, endorsement by, or official status from TiMi, Level Infinite, Microsoft or Xbox
- Reproduce game artwork or screenshots
- Advise on exploits, bugs or unauthorised mechanics
- Automate any in-game activity or interact with game servers
- Sell or trade in-game items or accounts

### Required disclaimer
Every AIGA product, document and public-facing page must include:

> "AIGA is an independent fan advisory service created by Network Grey. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with TiMi Studio Group, Level Infinite, Proxima Beta Pte. Limited, Microsoft, or Xbox Game Studios. Age of Empires and Age of Empires Mobile are trademarks of Microsoft Corporation."
