## What is the Mightiest Empire?

The Mightiest Empire (MEE) is Age of Empires Mobile's Kingdom vs Kingdom (KvK) event. Your entire server is matched against one other server. The event runs across four phases and lasts approximately 10 days in total.

Everything you do during preparation directly determines whether your server attacks or defends — and when. This is the highest-stakes event in the game. The rewards are significant. The consequences of losing are real.

---

## Event Structure at a Glance

| Phase | Duration | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| Matchmaking | 24 hours | Server is matched against an opponent. Bye round if no match found. |
| Preparation | 6 days | Both servers earn points across 5 stages. Winner becomes the Invader. |
| War | 8 hours | Invaders cross to the enemy server. Defenders protect their Imperial City. |
| Recovery | Limited window | Partial troop recovery from war stage losses. |
| Settlement | End of event | Final winner determined by total cumulative points. |

**If no opponent is found during matchmaking:** you receive a bye round. Rewards are sent via in-game mail at 12:00 AM the following Monday.

## PART 1 — FOR ALL ALLIANCE MEMBERS

*Read this section carefully. Follow every point. Ask your Governor if anything is unclear.*

---

### Before MEE Starts — Your Checklist

- **Heal all wounded troops** — you need every soldier available for the war stage
- **Do not use stamina** — save 100+ stamina for tribal elimination stage
- **Do not train troops outside of MEE** — save training speedups for the event
- **Do not use building or research speedups** — these are your biggest point lever
- **Do not promote troops during MEE** — *[Further info/confirmation required: whether promotion earns MEE points. Confirmed zero in AIGA data. Community reports conflict — verify in-game before acting]*
- **Do not spin the Legendary Wheel for fun** — save paid spins for MGE Day III, not MEE
- **Do not craft gear during MEE unless your Governor confirms Stage 5 timing** — gear forging contributes to power increase points on Stage 5

---

### Stage 1 — Resource Gathering

**Your job:** Gather as many resources as possible.

- Send your gathering march to the highest level resource node you can find the night *before* this stage begins
- Fill your march to maximum load — do not let it return to your city overnight
- When the stage resets, deliver that full load immediately — you earn points the moment it arrives
- Keep your gathering march running the entire stage without stopping
- Target level 7 nodes near the Capital where possible — they hold 5-6 million resources per fill
- If your mount has a "Full Stores" trait, equip it before the pre-load to maximise carry capacity, then switch to a gathering speed trait when the stage begins

**Gathering points are low compared to later stages. Do not burn speedups here. Just keep your march moving.**

---

### Stage 2 — Unit Training

**Your job:** Train as many troops as possible. Fresh training only.

- Only fresh training earns points — *[Further info/confirmation required: whether promotion earns points in MEE — see Governor section for detail]*
- Train the highest tier troop you can afford
- Keep your barracks running continuously — never leave queues empty
- Do not stop to promote troops during this stage unless your Governor confirms it earns points

**MEE training points per troop:**

| Troop tier | MEE points per troop |
|---|---|
| T3 | 70 |
| T4 | 100 |
| T5 | 160 |
| T6 | 280 |
| T7 | 500 |

**The higher the tier, the more points. Always train the highest tier you can sustain.**

---

### Stage 3 — Tribal Elimination

**Your job:** Kill tribes. Use all five marches. Burn all your stamina.

- Attack the highest tribe level you can hit
- Run all five marches simultaneously — rotate as stamina allows
- Stamina sources in order: daily allowance, limited-time assignments, temporary rest (+50), VIP store, saved stamina items
- Out of stamina? Park your marches on resource nodes and contribute to gathering passively

**Highest value tribes for MEE:**

| Tribe level | MEE points per defeat |
|---|---|
| Lv 25-28 | 20,800 |
| Lv 29-30 | 24,000 |

**Always hit the highest level available. Never waste stamina on low-level tribes.**

---

### Stage 4 — Speedup Consumption

**Your job:** Use building and research speedups. This and training are the two biggest point earners in the entire event.

- Use building and research speedups aggressively
- Do not use all your speedups at once — your Governor will tell you how to time this correctly for Stage 5
- 1 minute of speedup = 18,000 MEE points. This is 600x more valuable than in MGE
- 1 full day of speedups = 25,920,000 MEE points

**Do not use speedups casually outside of MEE. Every minute is worth 18,000 points.**

---

### Stage 5 — Power Increase

**Your job:** Gain power. Anything that completes gives power points.

- Let your queued buildings and research complete during this stage
- Troop training completing here also counts
- Crafting new gear contributes to power gain
- Do not go idle — keep queues running

**Your Governor will have timed your Stage 4 burns so that completions land here. Follow their instructions.**

---

### War Stage — What You Need to Do

**Before the war:**
- Heal all wounded troops
- Make sure M1 and M2 are fully staffed and ready
- Follow your Governor's instructions on where to position your city

**During the war:**
- Do not rally enemy player cities — this is a trap. One player on a test server lost 200,000+ troops rallying a city and nearly cost his server the event. Open field and Imperial City only
- Make sure each of your marches is manually set to fight its counter troop type — do not rely on auto-targeting
- Archers and Cavalry perform best in rallies at this stage of the game — prioritise those marches for alliance rallies
- Keep M1 and M2 available for alliance rallies at all times. Never let them sit idle during active war hours
- You earn points from taking losses too — valid cross-border battles score even if you lose the fight

**Troop tier rule:** Cross-border battles only score if your troop tier is within 2 tiers of the enemy.

| Your tier | Earns points vs | Safe from scoring vs |
|---|---|---|
| T4 and below | T4-T6 only | T6+ (they earn zero off you) |
| T5 | T3-T7 | — |
| T6 | T4-T7+ | — |
| T7 | T5-T7+ | — |

**Practical rule: T4 and below players are safe from being farmed by T7 enemies. Do not feel pressured to avoid the war stage if you are T4 or below.**

**One tier difference is manageable. Two tiers is a significant disadvantage — pick fights carefully.**

**Counter system reminder:**

| Your troop | Beats | Beaten by |
|---|---|---|
| Swordsmen | Pikemen | Archers |
| Pikemen | Cavalry | Swordsmen |
| Cavalry | Archers | Pikemen |
| Archers | Swordsmen | Cavalry |

---

### Recovery Stage — Do This in Order

1. **Do not press "Recover Now" yet**
2. Use your blue recovery tickets (earned during preparation stage milestones) — 10 tickets = +10% recovery rate
3. Ask all alliance members and friends to send you recovery assistance — each help increases your recovery rate further. There is a cap on how many assists you can receive *[Further info/confirmation required: exact assist cap and points per assist]*
4. Only press "Recover Now" after you have boosted your rate to the maximum possible
5. **This action is irreversible. If you press it early you cannot undo it.**

If you run out of time, the system will compensate unclaimed recovery according to your recovery rate and medal count via in-game mail.

## PART 2 — FOR GOVERNORS AND ALLIANCE LEADERS

*This section covers strategic decision-making, server coordination, and tactical execution. Read Part 1 first — everything there applies to you too.*

---

### Pre-Event: Server Unity

Internal civil wars are the single biggest self-inflicted weakness going into MEE. If your server spends the week before the event fighting itself, you will not have enough troops to invade or defend effectively.

**Actions required before MEE matchmaking closes:**
- Get all alliance leaders onto a shared Discord server
- Use a translator bot if your server speaks multiple languages — there is no excuse for skipping this in 2025
- Agree on a ceasefire for the duration of the event — internal enemies become allies
- Consolidate your server's strongest players into two or three alliances
- A server with condensed, strong alliances will consistently outperform one with many scattered, half-active groups

**Why condensing matters:** Rallies require full participation to be effective. If your best players are spread across six alliances, you cannot fill a rally with T6/T7 players. Condensing solves this.

---

### Pre-Event: Attack vs Defend — Choose Deliberately

This is the most important strategic decision of the entire event and most servers ignore it.

**The rule:** The server that wins the preparation stage sets the war timing based on their Imperial City schedule. If that time falls when your players are asleep, winning preparation hurts you.

**Before matchmaking closes, answer these questions:**
1. What time does your server's Imperial City typically open?
2. What time does the enemy server's Imperial City open?
3. Which time gives your server more active players during the 8-hour war window?

**If the enemy's Imperial City time is better for your server — deliberately lose the preparation stage.**

If you choose to defend:
- Do not lose preparation by more than 15% of the attacker's score — you receive 20% of their total prep points, so keep the gap as close as possible to stay competitive
- Defending has more structural advantages than attacking beyond the attacker's negligible 3% damage buff
- Defender counter-tactic: during the war stage, teleport behind enemy lines and siege player citadels. This forces Invaders to choose between continuing to push the Imperial City or porting back to defend their own cities — players who port back are locked out of the enemy server for one hour

---

### Preparation Stage: Timing the Stage 4/5 Overlap

This is the highest-leverage tactical decision in the preparation phase. Brief your top contributors directly.

**The method:**
1. Burn the bulk of your building and research speedups on Stage 4
2. Leave one significant build or research with 1-5 hours remaining as Stage 5 begins
3. Let it complete on Stage 5 — this captures power gain points from that completion on top of the speedup points already earned on Stage 4
4. After it completes, queue a smaller build or research and burn remaining speedups to continue scoring on Stage 5
5. Also: save any unupgraded low-level buildings specifically for Stage 5 — upgrading level 1-3 buildings costs zero or minimal speedups and gives free power gain points

**Communicate this timing to your alliance before Stage 4 begins. Members who burn all speedups on Stage 4 and leave nothing for Stage 5 waste significant points.**

### War Stage: Pre-War Setup

**Rank and early port-in:**

| Alliance prep rank | Early access (per side) |
|---|---|
| Rank 1 | 30 minutes before war start |
| Rank 2 | 20 minutes before war start |
| Rank 3 | 10 minutes before war start |
| All others | War start only |

Early rankings are calculated separately — top 3 Invaders and top 3 Defenders each get the privilege independent of each other.

**Tactical note on early port-in:** Players can temporarily leave their alliance and join the Rank 1 alliance before the port window opens to gain the 30-minute early access. This must be coordinated with the Rank 1 alliance leader in advance. Players cannot do this while cross-border — it must happen before crossing. Test server observations suggest this works but verify on live server before relying on it *[Further info/confirmation required]*.

**Positioning:**
- Use the early port-in window to position cities in optimal rally and garrison locations before the fight begins
- You cannot attack until the Imperial City officially opens — use this time for positioning only
- Place towers defensively if defending — towers slow enemy movement, damage troops passing through, and allow your own troops to dodge counters and retreat to cities

**Defenders: garrison setup**
- Garrison Sacred Towers matching your primary march troop type before the Imperial City opens — each grants +20% attack and defence to that troop type inside the Imperial City
- Keep constant reinforcements on Sacred Towers and the Imperial City — a rally with continuous reinforcements never fully collapses
- Put your strongest players at the front. T4/T5 players on the front line against T7 whales is a points donation to the enemy

---

### War Stage: Open Field Tactics

**Whale responsibilities:**
- Do not farm weak players — your job is to lock down enemy whales and stop them scoring
- Each march should manually select to fight its counter troop type
- Whale vs whale containment opens space for mid-spenders to farm points freely
- Enemy whales that cannot move freely will often teleport away rather than fight — this is a win

**Mid-spender and free-to-play role:**
- Condense into one or two strong marches — a 30M power player with one condensed march beats a 45M power player spread across four weak marches
- Once your whales contain the enemy whales, operate freely in open field
- Focus on positive trades — prioritise your counter troop type every engagement

**Attacking — positioning:**
- Port in as close to an enemy player hive as possible — you only have a limited active fighting window *[Further info/confirmation required: exact active combat window — Soldierboy states 90 minutes, full war stage is 8 hours — clarify whether 90 minutes is the Imperial City clash window specifically]*
- Ideal march travel time to first target: 1-2 minutes maximum
- Enemy towers near the port zone can be sieged down with siege units — do not treat them as a blocker

**What good looks like in open field combat:**
- Legendary gear at lv60+ (lv80 ideal)
- Star upgrades on gear (3-star minimum target for serious war performance)
- 5/5 red skills on primary heroes
- Tier 1 (gold) rings minimum — do not run Tier 0 rings against opponents who have Tier 1
- Red gems in primary slots

### Imperial City — Winning Condition and Strategy

**Critical rule:** Taking the Imperial City does not guarantee winning the event.

Imperial City capture gives your server 20% of the enemy's total war stage points added to your score. If the enemy's cumulative points across all stages still exceed yours, they win.

**Priority order:**
1. Maximise cumulative points across all five preparation stages
2. Maximise open field and rally scoring during the war stage
3. Use Imperial City capture to close a points gap if needed — not as the primary strategy

**If your Invaders win the Imperial City:** your appointed King rules both servers with full authority over officials, buffs, and reward distribution for the remainder of the event.

**If your Defenders win:** the enemy Imperial City loses its ruler and all officials — a significant operational blow that lasts until the event ends.

**Imperial City mechanics:** Same rules as your home server's Imperial City Showdown. Destroy gates, occupy, hold. Both sides must fight for it the same way — Defenders have no garrison advantage inside the structure itself.
