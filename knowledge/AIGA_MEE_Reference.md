## What is MEE

Mightiest Empire Event (MEE) is the alliance warfare event - different from MGE (Mightiest Governor) which is the individual player event. MEE involves coordinated alliance attacks and defences. Scoring is alliance-wide and individual contributions matter.

## MEE vs MGE Scoring Comparison

| Activity | MEE pts | MGE pts | MEE advantage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Defeat lv27 tribe | 20,800 | 260 | 80x higher |
| Train 1x T4 troop | 100 | 10 | 10x higher |
| Use 1 min speedup | 18,000 | 30 | 600x higher |
| Gather 100 resources | 10 | 1 | 10x higher |

Speedups are by far the highest leverage activity in MEE relative to MGE. If you have significant speedup stockpiles, MEE is the event to use them.

## MEE Strategic Guidelines

**Tribe attacks:**
- Always attack highest level tribes available
- Coordinate with alliance — rally attacks give more points than solo attacks
- Keep M1 and M2 available for alliance rallies at all times during MEE
- lv27 tribes at 20,800 pts each are highly efficient

**Troop training:**
- Save training speedups for MEE just as you would for MGE
- T4+ gives the best pts-per-resource ratio
- Coordinate with alliance to maximise alliance-wide training scores

**Speedups:**
- MEE is the highest value event for speedup usage
- 1 day of speedup = 25,920,000 points — extraordinary value
- Stack building and research completions during MEE active phases
- Do not use speedups casually outside of MEE/MGE events

**Gathering:**
- Run M5 gathering march continuously during MEE
- 10 pts per 100 resources is low but adds up passively
- Diao Chan gathering march contributes here without consuming stamina

## Preparing for MEE

Before MEE starts:
- Stock stamina (save ~100+ for tribe raids)
- Have training queue ready with resources pre-stockpiled
- Hold significant speedup reserves
- Ensure M1 and M2 are at full strength for rallies
- Coordinate with alliance on target priorities
- Heal all wounded troops — you need full march capacity
