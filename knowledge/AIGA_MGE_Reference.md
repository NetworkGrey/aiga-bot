# Age of Empires Mobile — Mightiest Governor Event (MGE) Reference
## AIGA Source Document v1.0

---

## Event Structure

MGE runs over 7 days. Each day has a primary scoring activity. Day VII allows all activities simultaneously.

| Day | Activity | Primary resource spent |
|---|---|---|
| I | Tribal Raid | Stamina |
| II | Hero Growth | Medals, Scrolls, Gear crafting |
| III | Gathering and Wheel | Resources, Wheel spins |
| IV | Race Against Time | Building/Research speedups, Forge materials |
| V | Troop Training | Training speedups, Resources |
| VI | Power Gain | All completions |
| VII | Go All Out | Everything |

---

## Day I — Tribal Raid Scoring

Points are earned per tribe defeated. Higher level tribes give more points.

| Tribe level | Points per defeat |
|---|---|
| Lv 1-4 | 50 |
| Lv 5-8 | 80 |
| Lv 9-12 | 100 |
| Lv 13-16 | 150 |
| Lv 17-20 | 180 |
| Lv 21-24 | 220 |
| Lv 25-28 | 260 |
| Lv 29-30 | 300 |

**Key rule:** Always attack the highest level tribe your marches can defeat to maximise points per stamina spent.

**Stamina cost:** 5 stamina per march. Sending 3 marches costs 15 stamina per attack.

**Example calculation:** Player attacks lv27 tribes (260 pts) with 3 marches using 100 stamina:
- 100 stamina / 15 per attack = 6 attacks
- 6 x 260 = 1,560 Day I points

## Day II — Hero Growth Scoring

| Action | Points |
|---|---|
| 1 Epic Scroll used | 350 |
| 1 Epic Medal used | 500 |
| 1 Legendary Scroll used | 2,000 |
| 1 Legendary Medal used | 2,500 |
| Craft 1 Rare gear piece | 1,000 |
| Craft 1 Epic gear piece | 5,000 |
| Craft 1 Legendary gear piece | 30,000 |

**Priority order by points per item:**
1. Legendary gear crafting (30,000 pts) — highest single-action value if materials available
2. Legendary Medals (2,500 pts each) — save all Legendary Medals for Day II
3. Legendary Scrolls (2,000 pts each) — save for Day II
4. Epic gear crafting (5,000 pts) — worth doing if forge queues are ready
5. Epic Medals (500 pts each)
6. Epic Scrolls (350 pts each)

**Strategic rule:** Never spend Legendary Medals or Scrolls outside of MGE Day II unless absolutely necessary. The 2,500 pts per medal is too valuable to waste.

---

## Day III — Gathering and Wheel Scoring

| Action | Points |
|---|---|
| Gather 100 Food | 1 |
| Gather 100 Wood | 1 |
| Gather 100 Gold | 1 |
| Gather 100 Stone | 1 |
| 1x Wheel spin | 1,000 |

**Key insight:** Gathering gives only 1 point per 100 resources. Wheel spins at 1,000 points each are 100,000x more efficient per action. Always prioritise wheel spins on Day III.

**Strategic rule:** Save all Legendary Advent wheel spins for Day III. Never spin outside MGE unless you have surplus.

**Gathering note:** Gathering is still worth doing passively on Day III since M5 gathering march runs continuously anyway. Just don't activate special gathering speedups for points — the return is too low.

---

## Day IV — Race Against Time Scoring

| Action | Points |
|---|---|
| 1 minute Building speedup used | 30 |
| 1 minute Research speedup used | 30 |
| 1x Fine Craft used | 2,000 |
| 1x Copper Sand used | 400 |
| 1x Silver Dust used | 1,000 |
| 1x Fine Gold used | 3,000 |
| 1x Meteor Steel used | 20,000 |

**Priority order:**
1. Meteor Steel (20,000 pts) — exceptional value but extremely scarce mount adornment material. Only use surplus.
2. Fine Gold (3,000 pts) — good value if available
3. Fine Craft (2,000 pts)
4. Silver Dust (1,000 pts)
5. Building/Research speedups (30 pts/min) — good if you have large queues running. 1 hour speedup = 1,800 pts.
6. Copper Sand (400 pts)

**Strategic rule for speedups:** Stack building and research completions to fire on Day IV and Day VI simultaneously. Start a long build or research on Day III evening so it completes during Day IV for double points (speedup points on Day IV + power gain on Day VI).

---

## Day V — Troop Training Scoring

**Critical rule:** Only FRESH TRAINING earns MGE points. Troop PROMOTION gives zero points.

This means:
- Train new troops during Day V for points
- Save all troop training speedups for Day V
- Do not promote during MGE — promote during peace periods instead
- Higher tier troops earn more points per troop trained

**Strategic rule:** Never use troop training speedups outside of MGE Day V unless troops are urgently needed for war.

*Exact per-troop point values vary by tier — verify at aoem-calculator.com/training before spending speedups.*

---

## Day VI — Power Gain Scoring

Points are based on total power increase during the day. All sources count:
- Buildings completing
- Research completing
- Heroes levelling
- Troops training/completing
- Gear upgrades

**Strategic rule:** Time all long-duration completions to land on Day VI. Start builds and research on Day IV/V so they complete on Day VI for maximum power gain points.

---

## Day VII — Go All Out

All Day I-VI activities earn points simultaneously. Use remaining resources here.

**Priority on Day VII:**
1. Any remaining Legendary Medals/Scrolls
2. Remaining troop training speedups
3. Remaining building/research speedups
4. Any remaining wheel spins
5. Tribe attacks with remaining stamina

## Common MGE Mistakes to Avoid

1. Promoting troops instead of training fresh during Day V — zero points for promotion
2. Spinning Legendary Advent wheel outside Day III — wastes 1,000 pts per spin
3. Using troop training speedups before Day V
4. Spending Legendary Medals on rank-ups outside MGE
5. Using building speedups randomly instead of saving for Day IV
6. Not stacking completions for Day VI power gain
