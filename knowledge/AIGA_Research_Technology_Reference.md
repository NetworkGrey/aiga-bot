## MILITARY TECHNOLOGY TREE

### Priority Order for AIGA Advice
1. **Deployable Troops 1** — unlocks 2nd march slot. Highest priority in the game.
2. **Deployable Troops 2** — unlocks 3rd march slot. *[not shown on wiki — verify cost in-game]*
3. **Troop Tier Unlocks** (T2, T3 per type) — gates troop training
4. **Attack researches** for your primary march troop type
5. **Hospital Capacity** — critical for war sustainability
6. **Defense researches** for primary march troop type
7. **Health researches** for primary march troop type
8. **Training Speed** — quality of life but not combat-critical

## ECONOMY TECHNOLOGY TREE

### Priority Order for AIGA Advice
1. **Building Speed** — directly reduces all building queue times
2. **Research Speed** — directly reduces all research times
3. **Food/Wood Gathering Speed** — supports gathering march efficiency
4. **Troop Load** — increases resources per gathering trip
5. **Villagers' Production** — passive income boost
6. **Production of Mills / Lumber Camps** — passive income boost
7. **Resource Protection** — protects stockpiles from raids

## Cross-Reference: VIP vs University Research Speed Bonuses

University Research Speed (lv5) = +5%
VIP Research Speed bonuses:
- VIP 8: +5%, VIP 9: +6%, VIP 10: +7%, VIP 11: +8%, VIP 12: +9%, VIP 13: +10%, VIP 14: +11%, VIP 15: +12%, VIP 16: +13%, VIP 17: +14%, VIP 18: +15%

All bonuses stack. A VIP 12 player with lv5 research has +14% research speed before any alliance assists.
