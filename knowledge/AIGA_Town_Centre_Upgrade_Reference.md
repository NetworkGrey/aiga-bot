## Why TC Level Is the Single Most Important Metric

- **Gates all other buildings** — no building can exceed TC level
- **Troop capacity** scales from 100 (lv1) to 40,000 (lv30)
- **Hero slots** unlock at TC12 (+1) and TC17 (+2) — enables 2-hero and 3-hero marches
- **Building unlocks** per level (see Buildings reference)
- **University** should always match TC level for maximum research speed
- **Events** — some require minimum TC level to participate

**Key milestone:** TC17 is the primary mid-game target. At TC17 you unlock 3 heroes per march, which dramatically increases combat power and gathering efficiency. Rush TC17 above all other goals.

## Key Observations

**Resource requirements per level:**
- TC1-6: Only Wood + Food (no Stone until TC5, no Gold until TC7)
- TC7+: All four resources required
- TC8 and TC9 oddly require no Gold — worth checking in-game
- TC15+: All four resources in roughly equal amounts throughout

**Build time acceleration:**
- TC1-6: Minutes
- TC7-12: Hours (1h 15min to 11h 39min)
- TC13-17: Near-daily builds (16h to 1d 17h)
- TC18-22: Multi-day (2d 3h to 6d)
- TC23-27: Weekly+ (8d 9h to 19d 17h)
- TC28-30: Monthly builds (24d to 44d 19h)

**TC30 is a 44-day base build time.** With University lv30 (35% research speed), Building Speed research lv5 (+5%), VIP 18 Building Speed (+15%), and 4 barracks all at lv30 — speeds don't apply to buildings the same way, but embassy assists and building speedups are how TC28-30 gets done in a reasonable timeframe.

## TC Hero Slots — March Hero Count

| TC Level | Additional Hero Slots | Total Heroes per March |
|---|---|---|
| 1-11 | 0 | 1 (commander only) |
| 12-16 | +1 | 2 heroes per march |
| 17-30 | +2 | 3 heroes per march |

**This is separate from Deployable Troops research which unlocks additional marches.** Hero Slots control how many heroes are in each individual march. Deployable Troops controls how many marches you can field simultaneously.

At TC12: 2 heroes per march (commander + 1 second)
At TC17: 3 heroes per march (commander + 2 seconds) — maximum

## Legendary Advent — Pack Discrepancy Note

Two sources give different pack sizes for Legendary Advent spins:
- **AIGA system prompt:** 10-spin pack for 4,200 EC (saves 4,800 vs singles at 900 each)
- **Community guide:** 5-spin pack for 840 EC each = 4,200 total

The total of 4,200 EC matches both sources. The question is whether it is 5 spins or 10 spins for that price. *[Verify in-game before spending]* — this affects value calculations significantly. If 5 spins for 4,200 EC, the savings vs singles (5×900=4,500) is only 300 EC. If 10 spins for 4,200, the savings is 4,800 EC.
