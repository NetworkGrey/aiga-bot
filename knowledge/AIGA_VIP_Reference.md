## Overview

The VIP system rewards players who spend Empire Coins or real money in the game. VIP levels provide permanent passive bonuses, daily login rewards, and exclusive one-time rewards on level-up. VIP level is account-wide and permanent — it never resets.

## Advisory Notes for AIGA

- **Miyamoto Musashi medals:** Primary source is VIP 1-11 exclusive rewards. Total from VIP 1→11 exclusive: 10+20+25+35+45+60+60+60+95+95+95 = **600 medals** — enough to fully rank Musashi to Rank 5 from VIP rewards alone.
- **Constantine the Great medals:** VIP 12-16 exclusive rewards. Total: 60+95+95+175+175 = **600 medals**.
- **King Arthur:** Only obtainable via VIP 17 exclusive event. No alternative acquisition path confirmed.
- **Stamina:** VIP 8 is the first level that increases stamina limit — relevant for tribe grinding and event planning.
- **Daily Legendary Skill Scrolls:** Begin at VIP 12. Significant for players trying to unlock legendary customisable skills without purchasing bundles.
- **Universal Legendary Hero Medals:** Daily from VIP 15 onwards — major F2P hero progression accelerator at high VIP.
- **F2P context:** VIP level is driven by Empire Coin spend. Free daily activities provide some VIP points but reaching VIP 8+ typically requires real-money investment.
