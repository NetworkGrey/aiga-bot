## University Research — Military Tree

Research is conducted at the University. The military tree unlocks troop tiers and improves combat stats. Higher University level unlocks more advanced research nodes.

### Troop tier unlock research
Each troop type requires its own tier unlock before that tier can be trained or promoted to.

### Combat stat research
Each has 3 levels. Covers Attack, Defence, Health for all four troop types plus All Units versions.

### Infrastructure research
| Research | Levels | Strategic importance |
|---|---|---|
| All Units Training Speed | 3 | Reduces troop training time |
| Hospital Capacity | 3 | Prevents permanent troop loss |
| Deployable Troops 1 | 1 | Unlocks additional march slot — critical |
| Deployable Troops 2 | 1 | Unlocks 5th march slot — critical |

**Deployable Troops research is among the highest impact unlocks in the game.** Prioritise both nodes as early as possible.

### Research priority guidance
- Early game: Economy and Hospital Capacity first
- Mid game: Troop tier unlocks for your primary march type
- Late game: Max combat stats for primary troop types

## Mercenary Camp Research

The Mercenary Camp is a separate research building providing additional combat bonuses beyond the University. Contains two tiers of research, each with 24 technologies across all four troop types.

### Research categories per troop type (both tiers)
Each troop type (Swordsmen, Pikemen, Cavalry, Archers) has 6 research nodes:
- Attack bonus
- Defence bonus
- Health bonus
- Counter damage up (damage to countered type)
- Counter damage down (damage taken from counter type)
- Damage bonus

Each node has 5 levels.

### Mercenary Camp Tier 1 — Costs per level

| Level | Resources (each type) | Research time |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 2,500,000 | 7d 17h |
| 2 | 3,750,000 | 11d 12h |
| 3 | 5,620,000 | 17d 7h |
| 4 | 8,120,000 | 25d |
| 5 | 11,200,000 | 34d 17h |

All four resources (food/wood/stone/gold) cost equally per level. Total to max one Tier 1 node: ~31M of each resource, ~96 days research time.

Total to max all 24 Tier 1 nodes: ~744M of each resource.

### Mercenary Camp Tier 2 — Costs per level

| Level | Resources (each type) | Research time |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 15,000,000 | 46d 7h |
| 2 | 22,500,000 | 69d |
| 3 | 33,750,000 | 104d |
| 4 | 50,600,000 | 156d |
| 5 | 33,750,000 | 104d |

Tier 2 research is extremely long-term. Tier 2 lv1 alone costs 15M of each resource and 46 days. This is end-game content.

### Mercenary Camp research priority

Focus research on your primary march troop types only. Spreading across all four troop types simultaneously is inefficient.

**For a Sword-focused player (M1 Swordsmen lead):**
1. Swordsmen Attack bonus lv1-5 (Tier 1)
2. Swordsmen Damage bonus lv1-5 (Tier 1)
3. Swordsmen Counter up lv1-5 (Tier 1) — boosts damage to Pikemen
4. Then Pikemen for M2

**Do not research:**
- Troop types not in your primary marches
- Tier 2 until Tier 1 is complete for primary troop type

## Legendary Advent Wheel — Complete Data

The Legendary Advent wheel is the primary source of hero medals. Costs Empire Coins per spin.

### Spin costs
| Option | Empire Coins |
|---|---|
| Single spin | 900 |
| 10-spin pack | 4,200 |
| Free spins per wheel | 8 |

The 10-spin pack saves 4,800 coins vs ten single spins — always use 10-spin packs when spending coins.

### Average medals per spin
The average medals per spin (MPS) is 0.3. This means:
- 10 spins yields ~3 medals on average
- 100 spins yields ~30 medals on average
- To reach Rank 3 (50 medals cumulative) from Rank 1: approximately 133 spins needed on average

### Medal milestone bonuses
Reaching cumulative spin counts on a single wheel grants bonus medals:

| Cumulative spins | Primary medals | Secondary medals | Empire Coins spent (paid spins) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 20 | 10 | 0 | 10,800 |
| 60 | 15 | 10 | 46,800 |
| 120 | 15 | 10 | 99,600 |
| 200 | 15 | 0 | 166,800 |
| 300 | 15 | 0 | 251,400 |
| 400 | 20 | 0 | 336,000 |
| 500 | 20 | 0 | 420,600 |
| 600 | 20 | 0 | 505,200 |
| 800 | 30 | 0 | 674,400 |
| 1000 | 30 | 0 | 843,600 |

### Strategic wheel rules
- Always use free spins daily — never waste them
- Always spin in 10-packs when spending coins for efficiency
- Save paid spins for MGE Day III (1,000 pts per spin)
- Do not spin just for medals outside of MGE unless targeting a specific milestone bonus
- Primary medals go to your highest-priority hero (M1 lead first)
- Secondary medals go to a secondary target

### Medals needed per rank (reminder)
Rank 1=10, Rank 2=20, Rank 3=50, Rank 4=100, Rank 5=150, Rank 6=270 (600 total)

To fully rank up one hero from scratch to Rank 6 requires 600 medals. At 0.3 MPS average that requires approximately 2,000 spins — roughly 1.8M Empire Coins in paid spins. This is a long-term investment for primary march leads only.
