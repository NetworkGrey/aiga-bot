## Gear Dismantling — Kits Returned

Dismantling gear returns Targeted Forge kits based on rarity:

| Rarity | Kits returned |
|---|---|
| Rare | 50 |
| Epic | 250 |
| Legendary | 600 |

**Rule:** Always dismantle Rare gear immediately — 50 kits returned is more valuable than an equipped Rare piece. Never dismantle Epic or Legendary without resetting first.

## Gear Milestone Levels — Strategic Importance

| Level | What unlocks |
|---|---|
| 10 | First gem slot (all slots) |
| 20 | Star upgrade available (requires duplicate piece) |
| 30 | Second gem slot (Head/Hands/Body), requires Legendary for most pieces |
| 40 | Third gem slot (Head/Hands/Body), Rare gear max level |
| 60 | Third gem slot (Legs only), Epic gear max level |
| 80 | Legendary gear max level |

**Priority rule:** Push all four pieces on a hero to lv10 before pushing any piece to lv20. Gem slots at lv10 give disproportionate value across all four slots simultaneously.

## Gem System

Gems are inserted into gear slots once unlocked. Three gem categories exist.

### Gem categories

**Strategy gems** — boost troop stats:
- Unit health
- Unit defense
- Unit attack
- Unit capacity
- Gathering speed

**Hero gems** — boost hero stats:
- Might
- Strategy
- Armor
- Siege
- All attributes

**Tactic gems** — boost skill damage:
- Passive skill damage
- Secondary strike skill damage
- Active skill damage
- Turn-based skill damage
- Healing effects
- Normal attack damage

## Gear Investment Priority Summary

1. Get all 4 pieces to lv10 on M1 heroes first — unlocks first gem slot on all pieces
2. Then push all 4 pieces to lv20 on M1 — unlocks star upgrades
3. Repeat for M2 heroes before touching M3/M4/M5
4. Insert gems as soon as slots unlock — even tier 1 gems provide immediate benefit
5. Never dismantle Epic or Legendary — reset to recover materials instead
6. Rare gear: dismantle immediately for 50 kits each

## Advisory Notes for AIGA

**Skill damage gear matters for skill-heavy heroes:**
- Miyamoto Musashi (Sword) — Flowing Force (Legendary hands) + Shadowless Wind (Legendary legs) provide skill damage. Both are confirmed on his current gear. Correct.
- Joan of Arc (Pike) — Ring of Chaos (Legendary hands) is the skill damage hands piece for pike. Her current Legacy Arm Armor does not have skill damage — upgrade path to Ring of Chaos is a priority for skill-damage builds.
- Guan Yu (Cavalry) — Dust-Free Boots (Legendary legs) confirmed in his current gear. Contract of Light hands — skill damage status unconfirmed *[verify in-game]*.
- Yi Sun-Shin (Archer) — Embrace of Effulgence is the skill damage hands for archers. Currently on Skyshatter Gauntlets (no skill damage). Upgrade priority.

**Counter damage stats are more valuable than they appear:**
- Head and Body gear reduce damage from the unit type that counters you
- Hands and Legs gear boost damage against the unit type you counter
- These stats are baked into all gear at every tier — not optional additions. They passively amplify the counter system's 30% damage differential.

**Elimination rate in siege:**
- Hands pieces note "base elimination rate during siege" — relevant for city attack players
- Legs pieces note "elimination rate up" (general)
- Low strategic value for field combat — siege stat category
