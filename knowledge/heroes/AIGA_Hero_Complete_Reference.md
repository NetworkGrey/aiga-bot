### Guan Yu
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Specialties:** Warrior / Attack / Critical Strike
**Unit type:** Cavalry
**Rating:** 4.3/5

**Base stats at lv1:**
Might: 99.26 (+0.81/lv) | Armor: 56.82 (+0.47/lv) | Strategy: 53.03 (+0.44/lv) | Siege: 29.48 (+0.24/lv)

**Commander skill:** Deals might damage to enemy troop (157.94% might bonus) and grants the hero 22.35% critical strike rate for 3s.

**Signature skill:** Increases the hero's critical strike rate by 9.54%. For the first 12s of battle, additionally increases critical strike rate by 13.41% (triggers once every 2m).

**Recommended skill build (Cavalry):** Dragon Blade / Heroic Berserker / Critical Insight / Weak Spot Attack

**Best pairings:** Lu Bu, Attila the Hun, Darius the Great, Rani Durgavati, Harald

**Available skills:** Dragon Blade, Gallantry, Infuriation, Armor Piercer, Fatal Blow, Weak Spot Attack, Berserker, Critical Insight

**Medal source:** Tavern

**AIGA assessment:** Strong S-tier cavalry lead. Critical strike focus creates explosive damage spikes. Pairs exceptionally well with Lu Bu for cavalry-heavy compositions. High priority for cavalry march players.

### Yi Sun-Shin
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Specialties:** Tactician / Attack / Gather
**Unit type:** Archer
**Rating:** 4.5/5

**Base stats at lv1:**
Might: 48.87 (+0.58/lv) | Armor: 51.52 (+0.52/lv) | Strategy: 80.45 (+0.82/lv) | Siege: 43.56 (+0.54/lv)

**Commander skill:** Deals strategy damage to up to 5 enemy troops in a fan area in front (113.24% strategy bonus). Reduces your troop's might damage taken by 4.47% for 9s.

**Signature skill:** Enters charging state. After 3s, deals strategy damage to enemy troop (68.54% strategy bonus) and increases damage of the next signature active skill activated by your hero with the highest strategy.

**Passive:** Increases gathering speed for all resources by 14.35% (upgrades to 15.38%).

**Recommended skill build (Archer):** Crashing Waves / Tidal Flood / Conflagration / War Tactic

**Best pairings:** Suleiman, Theodora, Queen Seondeok, Yuan Xia

**Available skills:** Crashing Waves, Tidal Flood, Efficient Harvest, Blaze Impact, Organized Retreat, Conflagration, War Tactic, Strategy Master's Gift

**Medal source:** Tavern

**AIGA assessment:** Dual-role hero — strong archer attack lead with Conflagration burn synergy AND a gathering specialist via the Efficient Harvest passive. Versatile. Can serve in M4 archer march or as gathering support. The Efficient Harvest passive makes him one of the few combat-capable heroes worth considering for gathering duty.

### Yodit
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Specialties:** Warrior / Attack / Active
**Unit type:** Swordsmen
**Rating:** 4.3/5

**Base stats at lv1:**
Might: 98.70 (+0.98/lv) | Armor: 79.68 (+0.83/lv) | Strategy: 69.19 (+0.72/lv) | Siege: 77.49 (+1.08/lv)

**Commander skill:** Deals 3 instances of damage to up to 3 enemy troops in a fan-shaped area (32.78% might bonus per hit). When 4 stacks of Deterrence are active, deals 5 instances instead. Grants 100 initial rage on entering battle.

**Signature skill:** Deals might damage to target (80.46-85.32% might bonus) and adds 1 stack of Deterrence. Each Deterrence stack increases hero's might by 8.94-9.48, up to 4 stacks. 2.98-3.16% chance to enter damage immunity state for 1s.

**Recommended skill build (Swordsmen):** Wrath Forged Blade / Shadow of Terror / Executor / Battle Roar

**Best pairings:** Miyamoto, Tribhuwana, Constantine the Great, Hammurabi

**Available skills:** Wrath Forged Blade, Shadow of Terror, Shield Slam, Crashing Boulder, Peerless Strike, Battle Roar, Executor, Furious Charge

**AIGA assessment:** Strong sword lead with stacking damage mechanic. The Deterrence system rewards sustained combat — builds into 5-hit commander skills with full stacks. Pairs well with Miyamoto as co-lead or support. Solid A/S-tier sword option for players who don't yet have Musashi or as M5 sword support.

### Toyotomi Hideyoshi
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Specialties:** Marshal / Attack / Active
**Unit type:** Swordsmaster
**Rating:** 4.2/5

**Base stats at lv1:**
Might: 82.41 (+0.77/lv) | Armor: 55.98 (+0.54/lv) | Strategy: 63.08 (+0.60/lv) | Siege: 74.42 (+0.76/lv)

**Commander skill:** Deals might damage to up to 2 enemy troops in a fan-shaped area (120.69% might bonus). Reduces the might of the enemy's highest-might hero by 17.88 and the strategy of their highest-strategy hero by 17.88 for 6s.

**Signature skill:** Deals 1 instance of might damage to target (80.46% might bonus) and increases the next signature skill damage of your hero with the highest might by 5.96%.

**Recommended skill build (Swordsmen):** Ape Rage / Gnawing Strike / Furious Charge / High Spirit

**Best pairings:** Tokugawa Ieyasu, Oda Nobunaga

**Available skills:** Ape Rage, Gnawing Strike, Shield Slam, Crashing Boulder, Peerless Strike, Bloodthirst, High Spirit, Furious Charge

**AIGA assessment:** Debuff-oriented sword commander. The commander skill enemy stat reduction (might and strategy debuff simultaneously) is particularly valuable in rally scenarios. Pairs tightly with Tokugawa and Oda Nobunaga — the Japanese trio synergy is well-established. Currently used in Uncle Gus's M5 war configuration.

### Otto
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Specialties:** Marshal / Attack / Buff
**Unit type:** Cavalry and Pikemen
**Rating:** 4.2/5

**Base stats at lv1:**
Might: 93.77 (+0.99/lv) | Armor: 83.7 (+0.70/lv) | Strategy: 70.84 (+0.71/lv) | Siege: 68.74 (+0.82/lv)

**Commander skill:** Deals might damage to up to 3 enemy troops in a fan-shaped area (113.24-174.80% might bonus). When your troop receives fatal damage, they enter "Divine Radiance" state — immune to all damage for 7s (cooldown: 2m). Grants 100 initial rage on entering battle.

**Signature skill:** Every 6s, deals might damage to an enemy troop (59.60-63.20% might bonus). Every 3 activations, reduces cooldown of next signature skill by 3s. Each signature skill activation increases damage dealt by all troops within range for 3m, up to 6 stacks.

**Recommended skill build (Pikemen):** Sacred Coronation / Imperial Privilege / Holy Roman Empire / Furious Charge

**Best pairings:** Octavian, Qin Shi Huang, Ram Khamhaeng, Julius Caesar

**Available skills:** Sacred Coronation, Imperial Privilege, Formation Attack, Change of Formation, Holy Roman Empire, Savage, War Elephant, Furious Charge

**AIGA assessment:** Excellent dual-type (cavalry/pike) lead with a unique survival mechanic — the Divine Radiance immunity is one of the few ways to prevent fatal damage in the game. The stacking damage buff via signature skill makes Otto better as a fight goes longer. Strong A/S-tier pike option to pair with Octavian.

### Vlad
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Specialties:** Warrior / Attack / Passive
**Unit type:** Cavalry and Pikemen
**Rating:** 4.5/5

**Base stats at lv1:**
Might: 104.2 (+1.02/lv) | Armor: 88.73 (+0.90/lv) | Strategy: 59.18 (+0.65/lv) | Siege: 76.81 (+0.80/lv)

**Commander skill:** Deals might damage to up to 2 enemy troops in fan-shaped area (216.20% might bonus). For 6s, reduces troop's damage taken by 4.60% and grants 40% chance to increase might damage to 1.09x. Grants 100 initial rage on entering battle.

**Signature skill:** When hero with highest might deals damage, 50% chance to also deal might damage to an enemy troop (44.24% might bonus, once per 3s). When this hero deals damage, 25% chance to ignore 4.74% of target's defence.

**Recommended skill build (Pikemen):** King's Edict / Dragon's Vigor / Deadly Dragonthron / Whirlwind Sweep

**Best pairings:** Cyrus the Great, Mansa Musa, Leonidas, Lu Bu

**Available skills:** King's Edict, Dragon's Vigor, Counterattack, Vigilance, Deadly Dragonthron, Battle Roar, Whirlwind Sweep, Forceful Retaliation

**Note:** Specific builds marked "Coming Soon" on source — skill build above is preliminary.

**AIGA assessment:** Highest base Might stat in this batch (104.2) with strong passive defence reduction on attacks. The damage reduction (4.60%) plus chance-based might damage multiplier makes Vlad a durable high-output cavalry/pike lead. Strong S-tier potential. The passive defence penetration is particularly valuable in high-armour matchups.

### Robin Hood
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Specialties:** Marshal / Support / Recovery
**Unit type:** Cavalry
**Rating:** 4.0/5

**Base stats at lv1:**
Might: 103.01 (+1.06/lv) | Armor: 82.48 (+0.83/lv) | Strategy: 76.43 (+0.65/lv) | Siege: 25.25 (+0.30/lv)

**Commander skill:** Deals might damage to enemy troop (147.20% might bonus) and increases activation chance of all heroes' signature secondary strike skills by 3.22% for 9s. When any hero activates a signature secondary strike skill, all heroes gain 6.9 might, up to 3 stacks.

**Signature skill:** Increases activation chance of commander's signature secondary strike skill by 2.38-2.53%. When commander activates signature secondary strike, recovers troop units (32.78-34.76% might bonus) and increases commander's signature skill damage.

**Recommended skill build (Cavalry):** Forest Phantom / Merry Companion / Protracted Battle / Peaceful Haven

**Best pairings:** El Cid, Saladin, Tariq

**Available skills:** Forest Phantom, Merry Companion, Armor Piercer, Fortification, Peaceful Haven, Pay in Blood, Protracted Battle, Righteous Judgement

**AIGA assessment:** Niche support hero built around secondary strike synergy. Best in a march where the commander has strong secondary strike skills (El Cid, Saladin compositions). The recovery + might stacking on secondary strike activations creates a self-sustaining combat loop. A-tier support for secondary strike-focused cavalry builds but not a general-purpose pick.

### Qin Shi Shuang
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Specialties:** Marshal / Buff / Recovery
**Unit type:** Swordsmen and Pikemen
**Rating:** 4.0/5

**Base stats at lv1:**
Might: 93.34 (+1.03/lv) | Armor: 73.0 (+0.70/lv) | Strategy: 84.33 (+0.97/lv) | Siege: 81.87 (+0.91/lv)

**Commander skill:** Recovers a quantity of units for your troop (239.20% strategy+might bonus) and increases skill damage by 8.28% for 9s. Grants 100 initial rage on entering battle.

**Signature skill:** Every 6s, recovers units (29.80-31.60% strategy+might bonus) and reduces troop damage taken by 2.98-3.16% for 3s. 10% chance to grant Damage Immunity for 1s.

**Recommended skill build (Pikemen):** Dragon's Share / Emperor of Eons / Protracted Battle / Peaceful Haven

**Best pairings:** Otto, Octavian, Caesar, Ram Khamhaeng

**Available skills:** Dragon's Share, Emperor of Eons, Formation Attack, Fortification, Protracted Battle, King's Blade, Triple Offensive, Peaceful Haven

**Note:** Specific builds marked "Coming Soon" on source.

**AIGA assessment:** Tanky recovery/buff support for Swordsmen or Pikemen marches. The recovery commander skill is among the strongest in the game (239.20% ratio). Pairs naturally with Otto and Octavian for a durable pike composition. Ideal as M2 support for a pike-heavy rally setup. High Strategy and Siege stats are unusual — makes Siege-type gem builds worth considering on this hero specifically.

### Zhuge Liang
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Specialties:** Tactician / Support / Buff
**Unit type:** Swordsmen and Archers
**Rating:** 5.0/5 (limited votes)

**Base stats at lv1:**
Might: 75.65 (+0.65/lv) | Armor: 81.78 (+0.78/lv) | Strategy: 104.13 (+1.13/lv) | Siege: 76.0 (+0.77/lv)

**Commander skill:** Reduces troop's damage taken by 6.90% (strategy bonus) and enters "Qimen" state — directly reduces might skill damage equal to 0.16% of deployed units, up to 80% damage reduction, for 6s. Grants 100 initial rage on entering battle.

**Signature skill:** Transfers 3.16% of his base attributes (using commander's highest attribute) to commander at 1.1x ratio. For first 12s of battle, reduces damage taken by troop. Every 3s, enters Qimen state for 1s — directly reduces might skill damage, with chance to recover units.

**Recommended skill build (Swordsmen and Archer):** Stone Sentinel Maze / Miracle of Qimen / Borrowing the East Wind / War Tactic

**Best pairings:** Ramesses, Charlemagne, Sun Tzu, Yi Sun-Shin

**Available skills:** Stone Sentinel Maze, Miracle of Qimen, Suppression, Sowing Discord, Conflagration, Borrowing the East Wind, War Tactic, Peaceful Haven

**Note:** Specific builds marked "Coming Soon" on source.

**AIGA assessment:** Exceptional defensive support. The Qimen state provides a hard cap on might skill damage (up to 80% reduction) which is one of the most powerful defensive mechanics in the game. Highest Strategy stat in this batch (104.13 at lv1). Dual sword/archer type makes him flexible. The attribute transfer signature skill uniquely buffs your commander rather than just the troop. Top-tier support for any march where the lead has high strategy. Potential S-tier support.

## Arabs Civilisation — Verified Data
*Source: Verified in-game by Gustav (Uncle Gus), March 2026*

| Bonus | Value |
|---|---|
| Cavalry Health | +2.5% |
| Food gathering speed in the world | +5.0% |
| Hospital Capacity | +2,500 |

**AIGA assessment:** The Arabs civilisation is the only one with a Hospital Capacity flat bonus — useful during heavy war phases where wounded troop capacity is a bottleneck. The food gathering speed bonus is the strongest resource-specific gathering bonus of any civilisation. The cavalry health bonus is modest but useful for cavalry-focused players. Overall a strong mid-to-late game civilisation for players who run heavy cavalry marches or consume large amounts of food through high-tier troop training.

**Status:** Arabs is confirmed as the 9th civilisation, listed as "NEW" on the official aoemobile.com site as of March 2026.
